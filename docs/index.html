Overview

The number of cumulative COVID-19 cases in operational and oversight countries in the region at the end of March 2021, stood at 16.6 M. This is an increase of 1.7 M compared with the end of February 2021. The two countries with the highest number of cases were India (12.2 M cases) followed by Indonesia (1.5 M).

Trends Over Time

Last Month’s Trends

In March 2021, compared with February 2021 there was a notable increase of average daily new cases in India (35,787 cases), and Philippines (5,514 cases). Meanwhile, there was a decrease in Indonesia (5,712 cases), and Malaysia (1,443 cases).
